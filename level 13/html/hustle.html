Hustle is a 2022 American sports comedy-drama film[2] directed by Jeremiah Zagar, written by Taylor Materne and Will Fetters, and co-produced by and starring Adam Sandler as a down-on-his-luck National Basketball Association (NBA) scout who dis covers a raw but talented basketball player in Spain (Juancho Hernangómez) and tri es to prepare him for the NBA draft. The film also stars Queen Latifah, Ben Foster, Robert Duvall, Heidi Gardner, and current NBA player Anthony Edwards. LeBron James produced the film through his SpringHill Company banner. Hustle was released in select theaters on June 3, 2022, and on Netflix on June 8.
